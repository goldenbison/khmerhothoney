About Us

OUR STORY

Khmer Hot Honey Origin

Khmer Hot Honey was born from a passion for Cambodian flavors and a desire to share the unique taste of our homeland with the world. Our journey began in a small kitchen in Phnom Penh, where we experimented with combining pure, locally-sourced honey with the perfect balance of chili heat.

Today, we pride ourselves on creating an artisanal product that honors Cambodian culinary traditions while bringing a contemporary twist to your favorite dishes. Each bottle of Khmer Hot Honey is handcrafted in small batches to ensure quality and consistency.

Our Process

1

Source

We carefully select raw honey from trusted local beekeepers.

2

Infuse

Our chilies are slowly infused into the honey to extract maximum flavor.

3

Balance

We perfect the balance between sweetness and heat for an unforgettable taste.

4

Bottle

Each bottle is filled and sealed by hand in small batches.
